About

Context

We began the project as a way to practice data journalism and were allowed to pick our own topic. I chose to focus on Greenhouse Gas emissions, originally looking to focus on emissions in just NYC before expanding to the entire country.

Data Pipeline

1. I retrieved data from The United States Environmental Protection Agency.

2. I put the data into google sheets in order to download it as a csv

3. I moved the data from google sheets into python and formatted it as a .json

Prototype

my originial prototype had me coloring in the states with greater greenhouse gas emissions using darker shades of blue

Feedback

my classmates reccomended that I add a highlight aspect to my graph to show the color and amount of greenhouse gas emissions

Takeaways

1. there is a wide range of greenhouse gas emissions per state

2. Mostly, industrialized states (Texas, California, etc.) produced a much higher amount of greenhouse gasses than rural states

All data from The United States Environmental Protection Agency.
